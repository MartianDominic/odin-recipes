Swedish Shrimp Salad Sandwich

Swedish Shrimp Salad Sandwitch

Description

Unsurprisingly for a coastal region, Scandinavian cuisine is laden with fish dishes.

This sandwich is a Swedish classic. Plump shrimp is tossed in a creamy dill and mayo dressing before being piled high on dark bread and served with hard-boiled egg.

Ingredients

For the dressing

  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 3 tablespoons sour cream, creme fraiche or plain greek yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons or more chopped fresh dill (save some for garnish)
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped onion
  • Dash of hot sauce
  • A generous squeeze of lemon
  • Salt and Ppper to taste

For the rest

  • 1 pound (500 grams) medium shrimp, cooked and peeled
  • 2 slices brown bread or pumpernickel or rye
  • 2 hard boiled eggs sliced
  • Jarred asparagus spears

    • Steps

    1. Stir the dressing ingredients in a bowl large enough to hold the shrimp.
    2. Taste and adjust the seasoning.
    3. Stir in the cooked shrimp and transfer to the fridge for a half an hour.
    4. To serve, use a slotted spoon to remove the shrimp from the dressing.
    5. Toast the bread, then arrange on a plate and spread some of the extra dressing on the bread.
    6. Arrange the sliced hard boiled eggs and asparagus on top then pile the shimp salad high.
    7. Garnish with dill. Serve with a knife and a fork
